Privacy Policy

Carvex — GCC Car Marketplace

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Overview

Carvex is a free iOS app that aggregates car listings from public marketplaces across the GCC region. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the data practices of this app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Carvex does not:

  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, camera, or other device features
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools

Advertising

Carvex displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data (ad interactions)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Carvex. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps.

Third-Party Services

Carvex fetches car listing data from publicly accessible marketplace websites (QatarSale, OpenSooq, q8car). These requests are made directly from your device. No user data is sent to these services.

Children's Privacy

Carvex is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
